## Objective
Teach Kalvin that MHALL (Mary Had A Little Lamb) is structurally equivalent to SVO (Subject-Verb-Object), so that Kalvin acknowledges MHALL == SVO and SVO == MHALL as a bidirectional countersign.

## Approach
Present the full MHALL == SVO countersign with nested subscript scaffolding in a single lesson. Kalvin must rationalise all identities, canonizations, and connotate entries from the subscript without prior scaffolding.
